Understanding Generalization in Diffusion Models via Probability Flow Distance
Huijie Zhang, Zijian Huang, Siyi Chen, Jinfan Zhou, Zekai Zhang, Peng Wang, Qing Qu
ArXiv / Website

We propose Probability Flow Distance (PFD), a theoretically grounded and computationally efficient metric to quantify distributional generalization in diffusion models. Using a teacher-student evaluation protocol, we empirically uncover several novel generalization behaviors.
